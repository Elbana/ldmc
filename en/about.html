Who We Are

Crafting Excellence
in Natural Stone

LDMC is the leading natural stone company in Laayoune, southern Morocco — the most trusted name in premium stone for over 30 years. Headquartered in the heart of the Saharan south, we specialize in luxury wall cladding, marble vasques, granite countertops, and fully custom stone solutions for residential and commercial projects.

With more than three decades of hands-on craftsmanship and an unwavering commitment to excellence, LDMC has earned its reputation as the go-to stone specialist across the region — transforming ordinary spaces into iconic statements of luxury and lasting elegance.

Trusted by architects, contractors, and interior designers throughout Morocco, LDMC delivers custom marble vasques, granite countertops, Calacatta marble bathrooms, stone staircases, and professional installation — all from our workshop in Laayoune.

Est. 24 July 2001  ·  Laayoune, Morocco
