Our Services

Full-Service Stone Solutions

Everything You Need,
Under One Roof

LDMC handles the entire stone project lifecycle — from selecting the perfect stone to professional on-site installation.

Custom Stone Cutting

Precise CNC and hand cutting of marble, granite, and travertine to your exact dimensions — vanities, countertops, cladding panels, and more.

Polishing & Finishing

Mirror-polish, honed, brushed, or leathered finishes applied by skilled artisans. We bring out the full natural beauty of every stone surface.

Bathroom Installation

Complete marble bathroom fitting — wall cladding, floor tiling, vanity installation, shower enclosures. Turnkey service from design to handover.

Kitchen Countertops

Custom-measured and installed granite or marble kitchen countertops, including sink cutouts, edge profiling, and backsplash panels.

Wall & Floor Cladding

Interior and exterior stone cladding — from feature walls and facades to full floor tiling — installed with precision and care.

Delivery & Logistics

Safe, professional delivery of stone slabs and finished products. We handle transport and on-site positioning with full care for fragile materials.
